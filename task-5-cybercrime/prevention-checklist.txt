Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
3. Always verify payment requests before approving
4. Use strong passwords for all accounts
5. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
7. Regularly check your bank statements for unusual activity
8. Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store
9. Do not respond to unknown calls claiming to be bank officials
10. Immediately report fraud to cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
